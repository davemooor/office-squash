THE CONSTITUTION

Rules

1. Match result

The player winning the most games wins the match. Results are entered as the number of games won by each player, for example 2–1. Draws are not accepted by the bureaucracy because somebody must eventually take responsibility.

2. Participation

Each player receives one participation point for every completed match, regardless of the result. This rewards actually arriving, playing and resisting the temptation to cancel because of a suspiciously tight calf.

3. League points

League points equal games won plus participation points. A 2–1 result therefore produces three league points for the winner and two for the loser: two or one game points, plus one point each for participation.

4. Ranking

Players are ranked by NRG Rating, which uses the Elo system. Beating a higher-rated opponent earns more rating points than beating a lower-rated opponent.

5. Elo settings

Ratings move after every confirmed result using a K-factor of 24. Olaf and Rich began slightly above the chasing group to reflect the existing office pecking order; from there, every result adjusts the ratings automatically.

6. Corrections

Incorrect results may be corrected by Dave's administrator login. When an old result is changed, the system recalculates every later Elo movement in chronological order so the current table remains consistent.
